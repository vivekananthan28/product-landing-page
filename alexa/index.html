Introducing Alexa Echo

Alexa Echo is a hands-free speaker you control with your voice. Echo connects to Alexa Voice Service to play music, make calls, answer questions, provide information, news, sports scores, weather, and more - instantly. All you have to do is ask.Alexa, play top Bollywood songs.
