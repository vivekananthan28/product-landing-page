Outstanding Core Features of The Product

Voice Control Your Music

Echo has a 2.5” downward-firing woofer and 0.6” tweeter powered by Dolby to deliver crisp vocals and dynamic bass throughout the room. If you are a Prime member, you already have access to Amazon Prime Music- just ask for your favourite artist or song, or request a specific genre or mood. You can also search for music by album, decade, or let Alexa pick the music for you.







"Alexa, Play Music everywhere".

Want to play music on an Echo device in another room? Now with multi-room music, you can tell Alexa to play the same song throughout your whole home. Just ask for a song, album, artist, or genre from Amazon Prime Music, JioSaavn and TuneIn. Simply use the Alexa App to create groups with two or more Echo devices by naming the group, such as “my home.” Once you've created the group, simply say “Alexa, play Bhangra in “my home.”

Alexa Communication

Introducing Alexa calling and messaging—a new way to be together with family and friends. Use your Echo device to call or message anyone who has an Echo device or the Alexa app. When you’re busy making dinner, just ask Alexa to place a call to Mom or send a quick voice message to a friend. You can also enable “Drop In” in the Alexa app for when you want to instantly connect with other Echo devices in your home, or your closest family and friends. Alexa calling and messaging is available within India at no additional cost—to get started, download the Alexa app on iOS and Android.

Pay your bills with Alexa

With Alexa, you can pay your utility bills - electricity bill, mobile phone, gas bill, internet bill, water bill and more - with one voice command. Just ask, 'Alexa, pay my bills."












Far Field Voice Recognition

Echo can hear you from across the room, even in noisy environments, or while playing music as it has seven microphones that use beam-forming technology and enhanced noise cancellation. When you want to use Echo, simply say the wake word “Alexa” and it responds instantly.
